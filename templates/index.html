Covid X-ray Classifier




Dos

  • If you have mild symptoms then you can stay at home and take treatment.
  • Isolate yourself in a separate room that has an attached bathroom.
  • If you have a low-grade fever then you can take paracetamol/Dolo.
  • Check your fever and oxygen levels at least 10-15 times a day. You can set a reminder for that on your mobile.
  • If the oxygen level is between 94-100 then you need not worry. If it drops below 94 then contact your doctor.
  • Have a liquid diet, easy to digest food, and drink at least 8-10 glasses of water.

Don'ts

  • Don’t take steroids or Remdesivir without a doctor’s prescription if you have mild symptoms.
  • If the symptoms are severe then stay in touch with your doctor from time to time.
  • If the viral load is high, you will get a fever for at least 5-7 days and your oxygen level will show a dip. So, keep checking the fever and oxygen level.
  • Do not google and take medication. Instead, speak to your family doctor.
  • Don’t take treatment at home if symptoms are severe.
